Gotham City

A Norwegian mercenary, Captain Jon Logerquist, founded Gotham City in 1635 and the British later took it over-a story that parallels the founding of New York by the Dutch (as New Amsterdam) and later takeover by the British. During the American Revolutionary War, Gotham City was the site of a major battle (paralleling the Battle of Brooklyn in the American Revolution). Gotham City is home to notable heroes, such as Batman, as well as notorious villains, like Joker.
